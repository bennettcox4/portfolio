About Me

I am a creative-minded, passionate student filmmaker and 3D animator who approaches problems with enthusiasm to learn. I am goal- oriented and excel both individually and as a part of a team in any role, whether I am leading the charge during a late night editing session or grabbing coffees for my colleagues on a film set. My career goal is to channel my talents and experiences in a way that benefits (or at least entertains) humanity as a whole.

Since starting my studies at the University of Pittsburgh, my classes have pushed me to create in many fields, including films, animations, games, programming, podcasts, videos, scripts, and music. I've worked every semester in our student union building's Office of Event Production as an Audio/Visual Technician. I've also done video editing work with local Pittsburgh companies Mercury Works and the August Wilson House, as well as for University of Pittsburgh's Communication Department and local Kappa Delta chapter. Recently, I was brought on as First Assistant Camera for a feature film being produced by Eli Zazu Productions titled "Little Rich Lives."
